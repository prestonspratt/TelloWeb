🛜 Connect to Tello Drone

    
DroneCode connects via a local proxy running on your computer. Follow the steps below.


    

      

        
1

        
Power on your Tello. Wait for the LED to blink yellow rapidly.

      

      

        
2

        
On your computer, connect to WiFi: TELLO-XXXXXX

      

      

        
3

        
Start the local proxy: run start-windows.bat or ./start-mac-linux.sh from the server/ folder you downloaded.

      

      

        
4

        
Enter your proxy address below. Default is localhost:8080 if running on this computer.

      

    


    

      
      
    

    

      
      
    


    

      ⚠️ The proxy must be running on a computer connected to the Tello WiFi. Your browser connects to the proxy via WebSocket, which then relays UDP commands to the drone.
